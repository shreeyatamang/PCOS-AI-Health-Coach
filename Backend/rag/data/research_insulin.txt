Source: Research Studies
Category: Insulin Resistance

Insulin resistance is a key factor in PCOS.

Key Insights:
- High insulin levels worsen hormone imbalance
- Early detection of insulin resistance improves outcomes

Mechanisms:
- Genetic and epigenetic factors affect insulin sensitivity
- DNA methylation may reduce insulin receptor function

Emerging Research:
- Long non-coding RNAs (LncRNAs) may regulate insulin signaling
- These could become future diagnostic markers

Conclusion:
Targeting insulin resistance through lifestyle and early intervention is critical in PCOS management.
