Source: Cleveland Clinic
Category: Treatment

PCOS treatment is personalized based on symptoms and goals.

Diagnosis includes:
- Medical history and symptom review
- Physical exam
- Blood tests (hormones, glucose)
- Ultrasound

Lifestyle Treatment:
- Low glycemic, high-fiber diet
- Regular exercise (150 min/week + strength training)
- Weight loss of 5–10% improves symptoms and fertility

Medications:
- Hormonal birth control regulates periods and reduces acne
- Metformin improves insulin resistance
- Anti-androgen drugs reduce hair growth and acne
- Fertility medications help ovulation

Advanced Treatment:
- IVF or other fertility treatments if needed

Note:
Treatment should always be guided by a healthcare professional.
