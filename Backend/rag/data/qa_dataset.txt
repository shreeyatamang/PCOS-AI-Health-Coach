Category: Q&A

Q: Why is my period late?
A: PCOS can cause irregular periods due to hormonal imbalance and insulin resistance. Stress, weight changes, and lifestyle factors can also contribute.

Q: What should I eat for PCOS?
A: Focus on whole foods such as vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and healthy fats. Avoid sugary and processed foods.

Q: Can exercise help PCOS?
A: Yes, regular exercise improves insulin sensitivity, supports weight management, and helps regulate hormones.

Q: What is the best diet for PCOS?
A: A low glycemic index or Mediterranean-style diet is often recommended for managing PCOS symptoms.

Q: Can PCOS be cured?
A: PCOS cannot be cured, but symptoms can be managed effectively through diet, exercise, and medical guidance.
